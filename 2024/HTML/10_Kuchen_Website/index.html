Kuchen Rezept

  • 280g all-purpose flour
  • 1 TL baking soda
  • 1 TL salt
  • 225g softened butter
  • 75g granulated sugar
  • 95g packed brown sugar
  • 1 TL vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 200-300g Chocolate
Step 1: Preheat oven to 375Â° F.
Step 2: Combine flour, baking soda and salt in small bowl.
Step 3: Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl until creamy.
Step 3 (cont.): Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Step 4: Gradually beat in flour mixture.
Step 5: Stir in chocolate.
Step 6: Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.
Step 7: Bake for 9 to 11 minutes.
Step 7 (cont.): Cool for 15 minutes.

Chocolate Chip Cookie

  • 280g flour
  • 1 TL baking soda
  • 1 TL salt
  • 225g softened butter
  • 75g granulated sugar
  • 95g packed brown sugar
  • 1 TL vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 200 Chocolate

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375Â° F.

Step 2: Combine flour, baking soda and salt in small bowl.

Step 3: Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl until creamy.

Step 4: Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.

Step 5: Gradually beat in flour mixture.

Step 6: Stir in chocolate.

Step 7: Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.

Step 8: Bake for 9 to 11 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes.
